Tools

Structured tools used in Into the Tank sessions and post-session analysis.

These tools are designed to support structured poker thinking—not replace it. They are used during live sessions and post-session breakdowns to help build, test, and refine decision-making.

Everything here is free to use. The tools are intentionally simple and evolving based on how they are used in sessions.

How the Tools Work Together

The tools are designed to support a full decision-making loop:

  • Build your own ranges (preflop decisions)
  • Assign and adjust opponent ranges (in-hand thinking)
  • Compare and discuss decisions during live sessions

Preflop Range Finder

A tool for building and exploring preflop opening and 3-betting ranges across positions and stack depths.

Use it to understand what hands belong in different preflop actions and to establish baseline assumptions before discussion.

Download Tool
Used in sessions to ground preflop decisions in structured range construction.

Range Assignment Tool

A visual tool for constructing an opponent’s range at any point in a hand by selecting specific hand combinations.

Use it to build, adjust, and compare villain ranges based on action taken and street progression.

Download Tool
Used during “tank time” to translate verbal reasoning into explicit range models.

Contributing

These tools are actively evolving as they are used in live sessions and post-session analysis.

If you’re comfortable with Excel or VBA and want to improve or extend them, feel free to build on them.
