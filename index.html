What This Is

Into the Tank is a collaborative poker study group focused on improving how we think through decisions, allowing us to make intentional decisions at the table.

Skill level is not the focus. The emphasis is on explaining reasoning, exploring different perspectives, and developing structured decision-making habits.

How Sessions Work

  • A hand is played in a live online environment
  • Play pauses at key decision points
  • The decision-maker explains their thinking
  • The group discusses alternative lines and assumptions
  • We continue through the hand step-by-step
