What This Is

Into the Tank is a collaborative poker study group focused on improving how we think through decisions.

The format is primarily oriented around live poker thinking, using online tools to simulate hands and study decisions in real time.

Skill level isn’t the focus. You don’t need polished answers—just a willingness to think through decisions, explain your reasoning, and engage in discussion.

How Sessions Work

Sessions run in a structured, pause-based format with a small group (typically 6–9 players) to simulate full-ring dynamics when possible.

  • A hand is played in a live online environment
  • Play pauses at key decision points (“in the tank”)
  • The decision-maker walks through their thinking
  • The group discusses alternative lines and assumptions
  • The hand continues step-by-step
